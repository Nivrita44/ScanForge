SCANFORGE - HOW WE BUILT YOUR BANGLA DOCUMENT SCANNER
=====================================================
A Simple Guide for Everyone (Non-Technical Version)

Imagine trying to read a book where all the letters are in the wrong order, and some words are missing their most important parts. That was the challenge we faced with these Bangla Voter List PDFs. Here is how we fixed it:

1. THE "REARRANGING THE BRICKS" PROBLEM (Solving Scrambled Bangla)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Bangla is a beautiful but complex language. In many old PDF files, the computer saves the letters in the order they APPEAR to your eyes, rather than the order they are typed. 

For example, when you see "বেগম" (Begum), the mark on the left (ে) is saved AFTER "ব". We built an "Automatic Logic Engine" that acts like a smart librarian. It looks at every word and says: "Wait, this mark belongs on the other side!" and shifts every vowel to its correct position.

2. THE "MISSING LINKS" (Fixing Complex Characters / Joint Letters)
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Some words use "Joint Letters." When converted to text, the links are lost. We taught the system to recognize these broken patterns and repair them automatically (e.g., repairing "Mastari" or "Mannan").

3. THE MODERN "PORTAL" DASHBOARD
--------------------------------
We transformed the interface into a professional "Bangla Digital Portal." 
- It now starts with a "Services Grid" (সেবাসমূহ), making it feel like an official government portal.
- You can jump between different tools (Voter PDF or NID Scanner) seamlessly.

4. INSTANT CAMERA SCANNING
--------------------------
No need to take a photo on your phone and then upload it. 
- Click the "ক্যামেরা" (Camera) button to open your device's camera inside the app.
- A scanning frame and laser line help you align your card, then you snap a photo for immediate processing.

5. DUAL-SIDE NID SCANNING
-------------------------
NID cards have info on both sides. 
- ScanForge now lets you scan BOTH the front and back.
- It automatically combines the text from both sides to give you a complete profile (Name, Address, Blood Group, etc.) in one go.

6. MOBILE FRIENDLY (Anytime, Anywhere)
--------------------------------------
The system is fully optimized for mobile phones. Whether using a laptop or a small smartphone in the field, the buttons, forms, and camera view stay perfectly aligned.

7. READY FOR EXCEL
------------------
- We added a "Shield" around Voter IDs to stop Excel from turning them into shorthand numbers.
- We added a "Secret Code" (UTF-8 BOM) so Bangla text appears clearly in Excel without strange symbols.

SUMMARY
-------
ScanForge is now a comprehensive "Smart Portal." It combines intelligent Bangla parsing, real-time camera scanning, and a professional, mobile-friendly interface to handle all your document extraction needs—from thick voter list PDFs to individual NID cards.
